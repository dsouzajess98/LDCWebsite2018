Terms and Conditions of
The Book Club

Rules and Regulations

  • Subscription Fee of ₹50/- per year for all students.

  • Caution Deposit of ₹200/- will be taken for each book and will be returned back once the book is returned in original condition and on time. The caution deposit will be returned at the end of the semester.

  • Only one book per member will be issued at a given point.

  • Book must be returned/renewed atmost 21 days after issue.

  • Fines will be deducted from caution deposit as follows:

    • For a stain: ₹15/-

    • For a tear: As per severity, between ₹40/- and ₹70/-

    • Return after due date: ₹10/- for the first week and ₹20/- for the following weeks

    • Misc. charges might be levied if the book is returned in a worse condition than it was issued as per the discretion of the book keepers.

Benefits for LDC members

  • No caution deposit.

  • Members will be allowed to read the books in the club room for free after prior information is conveyed to the book keepers.

Keep in mind!

  • ALWAYS use a bookmark to easily access where you left off. Avoid folding the pages.

  • Avoid creasing the spine of the book to increase its longevity.
