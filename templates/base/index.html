Message from the GenSec

Words are the most powerful force of nature. They make. They break. They burn.
They cleanse. They erase. They etch. They inspire!

We, at the Literary and Debating Club, NITW believe that the skill to communicate and the ability to express are fundamental to empower the young to become engaged citizens, skilled professionals and honourable leaders in this global society. We aspire to connect, support and inspire a diverse community of members of the club, the institution and all students at large to foster excellence, a dedication to diligence and a commitment to be tolerant & inclusive, through ❮competitive and otherwise❯ literary, speech and debate activities. The Club sees an initiation of focus on strengthening our skill set and letting each one of its members discover and indulge themselves in their passion whilst always providing a platform to showcase and learn for anyone with a zest for language,verse and good argument !

We strive to make the club an epitome of learning and growth; as our motto goes - Semper Anticus - we shall always strive to move forward !

About Us

Literary and Debating Club (LDC) aspires to be a platform where every individual is nurtured to realize his/her potential, where there is an atmosphere of joyous learning, responsibility to society, dedication to hard work, commitment to be tolerant, compassionate and inclusive.

Our Journey

The Literary and Debating Club had launched its journey at the start of 2016. The naissance of the LDC from the backbone of LnD showcases our evolution and progress. It’s a society of a number of talented, enthusiastic, devoted and hard working members and coordinators. The LDC has been continuously organising and participating in various debate and literary championships on an intra as well as an inter-college platform. From the very beginning of its journey in 2008, it has been established as one of the most progressive and popular clubs in the literary and debate arena.

This year we have remodelled ourselves via compartmentalization of members much similar to how multiple gears work cohesively to ensure smooth functioning.

Since the advent of the Book Club, Humans of NIT Warangal and the Online Blog, we have successfully established the first ever haven for novels and bookworms on campus, we have brought out different perspectives and experiences to the forefront, and have developed an audience base for interviews, reviews and stories.

Corporate Dodgeball

Venture further into a world where courtesy and diplomacy are the norms.


Fundamentally a combination of group discussion and a representation of an institution to a scenario posed by a certain jury, only quick wit and reasoning can help the participant. The scenarios are curated to be very diverse, interesting and simple enough for everyone to understand and participate, yet exhilarating enough to feel the pressure of the character and the jury.

Sway the jury, save yourself!


Guess The Book

Books have the ability to transport one into worlds unknown, mysterious and adventurous.


Paying a tribute to all the classics as well as the recent masterpieces, participants were required to guess the name of the book from merely a series of emoticons and send their answers to the facebook page. It was conducted on an online platform and received overwhelming response.

Open Air Debate

Debating has always been an age old tradition and passion of every speaker, opinionist and human.

We at LDC bring these discussion/arguments that we have with our friends, families etc onto a controlled platform governed by proper rules and regulations in the form of a formal debate. With the debate being held at the heart of our campus, Times Square, a different level of enthusiasm and debate was experienced.

So, pull up your socks, clear your throat and

Bring your A game to TS!


Just a Minute

Just a minute caters to the excessively long, elaborate speakers who just don’t know when to stop.

The event is an exhilarating competition of time. Testing which participant can speak about the most random topic given on spot, without hesitation, repetition and getting caught by the other contestants.

First one to reach a minute wins!


Pulp Fanfiction

A mix and match of your favourite fictional worlds with a pinch of reality!

This creative writing event aimed to get all those story-telling juices flowing.The participants expressed great talent in weaving stories of these fan-favourites in the most unexpected situations.

Best place to get your creative gears turning


Lantern 2.0

The staple Freshers' Event, presented after a revamp, to take fun and competition to the very next level.

As part of the first round, the participants contested in a treasure hunt spanning the entire campus of NITW. Filled with brain teasers and riddles galore, the completion of the course required quick thinking, keen observational skills and an insane passion for all things awesome! The next rounds tested the writing and speaking skills, through some innovative competitions such as Weave a Web, Shipwreck and Switch.
